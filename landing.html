Common questions

How is this different from ChatGPT or other AI tools?
ChatGPT is a chatbot — you ask it something, it answers. What we build are workflows: they run in the background, on schedule, taking action. They capture leads while you sleep, send reports before you ask, follow up without being told. The chatbot waits for you. The workflow doesn't.
Do I need to be technical to use this?
No. Once we build a workflow, it runs on its own. You don't open it, configure it, or troubleshoot it. We handle all of that. Your job is to do the work the business was built to do — ours is to run everything else.
How long does it take to see results?
First workflow live in 14 days from kickoff call. Measurable results in your business within 30 days. If you don't see a clear, quantifiable improvement in the first 60 days, we work for free until you do.
What if I'm too small for this?
If your business is doing under $300K a year, you probably don't need us yet. The bottleneck at that stage is usually lead-gen and sales, not systems. We'll tell you that on the call honestly. If you're between $300K and $5M, you're in our sweet spot.
What if I tried AI before and it didn't work?
You probably tried a generic tool, by yourself, for free. That's like buying a hammer and a saw and expecting a finished house. The work isn't in the tools — it's in setting them up to actually run your business. That's what we do.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Month-to-month, no contract, cancel with 30 days notice. We don't lock anyone in. If we're not delivering value, we shouldn't have your money.
